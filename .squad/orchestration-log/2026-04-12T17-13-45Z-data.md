## Key Findings Summary

- **Geocoding:** Photon/Nominatim for MVP → self-hosted or commercial for production
- **Access Rights:** UK PROW system well-covered in OSM; Scotland has Right to Roam (different model)
- **Seasonal Closures:** Not in static datasets; would require partnerships or UGC
